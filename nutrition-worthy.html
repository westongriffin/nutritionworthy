Hi, I'm Shyler

Nutrition changed my life. Let it change yours.

A Celiac Disease diagnosis at 20 sent me searching for answers — and what I found reshaped everything. Food became my medicine, my energy, and eventually my career. I earned my Master's in Integrative and Functional Nutrition, co-founded Alive & Well Kombucha, and built Nutrition Worthy to share what I've learned.

"Dream it. Build it. Choose habits you're excited to enact daily — until healthy feels like second nature."

Everything here is designed to be self-serve: courses you can take at midnight in pajamas, meal plans that decide dinner for you, and recipes that shop for themselves. Because you are worthy of feeling healthy and happy — on your schedule.
